${DoctorName} appointment notification for ${PatientName}

Dear ${CaregiverName},

This is an update regarding ${PatientName}'s appointment with ${DoctorName} on ${startDate} at ${startTime}. You can view your appointment details here: ${AppointmentUrl} .

Thank you,
${DoctorName}

Appointment Reminder

Appointment reminder is a notification sent to the patient about an approaching appointment.

${DoctorName} appointment notification for ${PatientName}

Dear ${CaregiverName},

This is a reminder that ${PatientName} has an appointment with ${DoctorName} coming up on ${startDate} at ${startTime}. Please confirm it or cancel at your earliest convenience: ${AppointmentUrl}.

Thank you,
${DoctorName}

${OfficePhone}
${OfficeEmail}

Patient Recall

Patient recall is a message sent to the patient to remind them to schedule a new continuing care appointment.

It is time for ${PatientName}'s regular check-up or continuing care appointment with ${DoctorName}.

Dear ${CaregiverName},

It is time for ${PatientName}'s regular check-up or continuing care appointment with ${DoctorName}. Please call us at ${OfficePhone} or visit ${ProfileUrl} to schedule your next appointment at your earliest convenience.

Thank you,
${DoctorName}

${OfficePhone}
${OfficeEmail}

Appointment Request Acknowledgement

This is an automatic notification sent to a patient to acknowledge their appointment request made through patient self-service.

${DoctorName} appointment for ${PatientName} on ${startDate}

Dear ${CaregiverName},

Your appointment for ${PatientName} with ${DoctorName} on ${startDate} at ${startTime} has been scheduled. You can view your appointment details here: ${AppointmentUrl} .

Please note that your request has not yet been accepted by ${DoctorName}'s office. You will receive a separate confirmation soon.

Thank you,
${DoctorName}

${OfficePhone}
${OfficeEmail}
